Electrical Company Templates

These sites all have a number of things in common:

They are all highly optimized, meaning they have much faster loading times than the vast majority of other company websites!

They are all highly customizable, sections can be added or removed easily.

They all provide ample space and opportunity to tell your potential customers about your company, and the services you provide.

Having an up-to-date, modern website makes businesses look more legitimate and professional, and greatly improves sales.

Template 1

This template features a short about-us section with brief lists of your main services, and a section for reviews. In the middle are some counters for milestones you have achieved, but this can also be replaced with something else, for example introductions for some of your team members. It also features several sub-pages, where you can go into more detail about the services provided by your company.

Template 2

This template starts with a slideshow featuring your main services, then a short about-us section, followed at the end by a reviews section. This page also has sub-pages to go into more detail about what your company has to offer.

Template 3

The third template starts with a list of your main services, followed by an about-us section that tells the potential client more about your company. This page does not feature any sub-pages, but like all other pages, sections are easy to add if more space is needed.
